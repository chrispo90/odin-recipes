Best Jambalaya

A spicy jambalaya with chicken, andouille sausage, rice, and Cajun seasonings that's easy to make in one pot.

Ingredients
  • Oil
  • Sausage
  • Chicken
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Vegetables
  • Rice
  • Broth
Steps
  1. Season the sausage and chicken pieces with Cajun seasoning. Sauté the sausage until browned, then remove with a slotted spoon and set aside. Sauté chicken until lightly browned on all sides. Remove with a slotted spoon and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté the onion, celery, bell pepper, and garlic until tender. Stir in the crushed tomatoes and seasonings. Add the meat and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in the rice and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, and cook until all the liquid is absorbed.
